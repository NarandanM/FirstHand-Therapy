ABOUT US

Who We Are

At Firsthand Therapy, our mission is to restore, provide, and improve total body function, health, fitness, and lifestyle changes for our patients and performing artists in the South Florida region by addressing pain, discomfort and the cause of the injury while educating, inspiring and engaging our community. Our commitment to the arts is reflected in the high quality individualized care provided by our therapy team. We want to make sure our clients continue to perform and express themselves within the community in the form they love the most.


Our interdisciplinary team members each complete an in-service topic related to their specialities every 6 months. We also provide information on the new changes to the profession and have our team attend the yearly CSM conference. Each therapist will lead a round table about certain cases they feel that are unique or rare and would be beneficial to the team. We would do continuing education as a team on topics that would help improve our knowledge on providing the best treatment for our patients. We also meet clients one on one and forage relationships with them that can transition into a lifelong partnership and referral source. We volunteer our physical therapy expertise at local events. We have a diverse population in the clinic, and our services reflect our reputation within the community. We also offer a discounted price for those who are affiliated with the New World Symphony group. Aside from our conventional therapy services, we will also sell products that will be beneficial for the patient’s HEP. These products include foam rollers, Theraband resistance bands and flex bars, Digi-flex’s, hand webs, resistance putty, Tiger Balm, and Bio Freeze.
