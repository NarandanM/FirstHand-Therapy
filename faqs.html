Frequently Asked Questions

Q: "When do I need to see a therapist"?
A: You will usually be reffered to a physical therapy clinic by another helthcare provider. In most cases, after an injury, or being diagnosed with certain conditions may lead you to seek help from a physical therapy clinic. If you live in Florida, there is direct access and you do not need a refferal.

Q: "What insurance is accepted at FirstHand Therapy"?
A: Cigna, United HealthCare, AMBetter, Blue Cross Blue Shield, TriCare, AvMed, Humana, Medicare, and Workers Compensation Insurances.

Q: "What if I dont have insurance"?
A: Patients may pay with Debit/Credit and cash is accepted.

Q: "What happens if I miss my appointment"?
A: You may reshcedule a visit by calling the office or you can make a new appointment on our website.

Q: "What if I dont speak English"?
A: We have staff on site who speak multiple languages including Spanish and Creol.

Q: "What if I think I have Covid-19"?
A: If you think you have any Covid-19 like symptoms, we ask that you seek medical attention and discontinue your visits until you have been tested and recieve negative results and/or quarantined for at least 14 days. Please and Thank You!
